Features

Utlizing the OpenFDA API, InvestMed allows you search OpenFDA for the 10 most recent drug and device recalls and device premarket approvals (PMAs). By using the AlphaVantage API, InvestMed allows investors to see if there is a correlation between stock price and recalls or PMAs.

OpenFDA Search

Search drug recalls:

  • by drug
  • by company

Search device recalls and PMAs:

  • by device
  • by company

Stock Correlation

Graph stock prices with openFDA data by puting in a company and its respective stock ticker symbol.
